Cui Bono?

This is the compilation of books I have read and liked. These books spread across an wide array of topics. My aimless meanderings across multiple subjects may resemble a cobweb spun by a drunken spider. Yet, I have thoroughly enjoyed this journey. These books have also vastly enhanced my ability to bore people to death over a cup of coffee. This website is my vainglorious attempt to handhold those who are looking forward to embark on a similar journey.
